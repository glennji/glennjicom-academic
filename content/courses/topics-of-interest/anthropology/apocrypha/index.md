The _apocrypha_ are books deleted from the Abrahamic old testament:
 - Tobias
 - Judith
 - Jubillees - mentions the nephilim
 - Enoch - mentions the nephilim
 - Esther
 - Baruch
 - Sirach
 - Maccabees
